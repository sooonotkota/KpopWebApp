LE SSERAFIM, a K-pop girl group under HYBE Labels, was formed with members who already had experience in the industry, including former IZ*ONE members Kim Chaewon and Sakura. The group debuted on May 02, 2022, with a bold and fearless concept, reflected in their name which is an anagram for "I'm fearless".
