Hybe Corporation

Hybe Corporation, formerly known as Big Hit Entertainment, is a South Korean entertainment company founded in 2005. The company operates as a record label, talent agency, music production company, event management and concert production company, and music publishing house. Hybe is best known for managing the globally successful boy band BTS, but it has expanded its portfolio to include other popular acts like LE SSERAFIM, TOMORROW X TOGETHER (TXT), and ENHYPEN.
